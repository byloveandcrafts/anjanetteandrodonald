Attire

Dress Code

✦
For the Gentlemen
Formal Attire
Navy blue, slate blue, or dusty blue suits & barong. Gold accessories are welcome. Crisp white shirts with matching ties or bowties. Barong Tagalog ensembles are warmly welcomed.
For the Ladies
Formal Attire
Blush pink, rose, mauve, or dusty blue formal gowns and cocktail dresses are encouraged. Please avoid wearing white or ivory.

Please Take Note

Notes & Reminders

✦
👥
Can I Bring a Plus One?
Due to space and seating constraints, we kindly ask that only the people listed on the invitation be present to ensure every guest has a comfortable experience.
🔒
Privacy Note
This is a small and intimate wedding. We kindly ask you to refrain from spreading our invitation. Only the person closest to us will be in attendance to our exchange of vows.
📵
Unplugged Ceremony
We truly appreciate the genuine happiness you feel with us. Please turn off or silence your phones and cameras during the ceremony.
⏰
Be on Time
Please arrive on time so we can begin the celebration promptly. Your presence means everything to us!
🎁
Gift Guide
With all we have, we've been truly blessed. Your presence and prayers are all that we request. But if you desire to give nonetheless, a monetary gift is one we suggest.
